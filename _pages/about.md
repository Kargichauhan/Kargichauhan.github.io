# Hello!!! 🚀  

I’m **Kargi Chauhan** — a Machine Learning Engineer, cosmic dreamer, and matcha enthusiast on a mission to push the boundaries of technology and imagination.  

My journey began at the **University of Arizona**, where I earned a degree in Information Science with a focus on Machine Learning and Data Science. Today, I’m at the forefront of generative AI, working with cutting-edge **Large Language Models (LLMs)** at 3UM. My work isn’t just about technology; it’s about creating tools that transform how we connect, learn, and innovate.  

---

## The Why Behind What I Do 🌟  

I believe in making AI not only **powerful** but also **explainable**. My research in **Explainable AI (XAI)** fuels this passion, helping to demystify AI and make it accessible to everyone.  

My dream? To bridge the gap between human curiosity and AI’s incredible potential, ensuring the future of technology is inclusive, transparent, and empowering.  

But my interests don’t stop there. The intersection of **space exploration** and **AI** lights up my imagination. From visualizing the cosmos to dreaming up AI-driven space missions, I’m constantly inspired by the mysteries of the universe.
